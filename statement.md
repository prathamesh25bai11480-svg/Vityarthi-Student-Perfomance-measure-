Problem Statement
Many students are struggle to understand why their academic performance fluctuates is  and what factors influence their marks. Teachers also find it difficult to identify students who might need additional support before exams. There is  an often no simple, data-driven way to estimate how a student will perform based on their study habits, attendance, sleep routine, and time spent online.
This project aims to build a practical system a that can look at these everyday habits and predict a student’s performance in a straightforward and understandable  way. The goal is not just a prediction, but also helping students become morebe  aware of the behaviours that affect their learning outcomes

Scope of the Project

The project focuses on the  developing a machine-learning based model that predicts student marks using a small but meaningful set of learning-related features.The work includes creating a clean dataset, training and of comparing different prediction models, analysing the importance of individual factors, and presenting results in an easily interpretable form. The scope is limited to prediction using four main inputs  : study hours, attendance, sleep hours, and daily internet usage.
While the system a does not include a full-scale web or mobile application, the prediction pipeline and analysis can be extended into such systems later.

Target Users 
Studnts who wants to estimate their in  expected performance and understand which habits influence their marks the most.
Teachers and mentors looking for aquick, data-driven way to identify learners who may require additional guidance.
Parents who want clarity on how daily routines relate to academic outcomes.Educational institutions exploring early-warning systems for better academic planning.

High-Level Features
Prediction of student marks based o four behavioural and academic inputs.
Visual graphs are that compare predicted results with real values for easier interpretation.
Feature importance analysis that highlights which habits contribute most to performance.Clean and simple workflow that prepares data, trains models, evaluates them, and shows results clearly Expandable design, allowing future integration with real-life student data or deployment into an app or dashboard .
